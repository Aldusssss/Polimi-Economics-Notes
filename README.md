# Polimi-Economics-Notes

📖 **Course Content Structure**

The notes are organized into 5 main macro-areas, covering the fundamentals of business economics from corporate governance to investment evaluation. These notes are based on lectures from the first-year Computer Engineering course at Politecnico di Milano.


## 🏢 **[01 — The Firm and Corporate Governance](./Lessons-1)** (1.0, 1.1, 1.2, 1.3)


<details>
<summary>Main topics</summary>

<br>

- Nature and definition of the firm (for-profit, non-profit, cooperative)
- Legal forms (partnerships vs. corporations)
- Economic subjects (shareholders, stakeholders)
- Corporate governance (General Meeting, Board of Directors, Board of Auditors)
- Governance models (traditional, dualistic, one-tier)
- Separation between ownership and control
- Startups and spin-offs
- Remuneration of involved subjects
- Shareholder rights

</details>

## 📈 **[04 — Financial Ratio Analysis](./Lessons-4)**
(2.7-2.8)

<details>
<summary>Main topics</summary>

<br>

- ROE (Return on Equity)
- ROI (Return on Investment)
- Financial leverage and cost of debt (R)
- Liquidity and solvency ratios

## 🧮 **[05 — Managerial Accounting and Investment Evaluation](./Lessons-5)**
(3.0-3.5, 4.0-4.7)

<details>
<summary>Main topics</summary>

<br>

**Costing:**
- Cost classification (direct/indirect, fixed/variable, product/period)
- Direct costing vs. Full costing
- Traditional techniques (Process, Job Order, Operation Costing)
- Activity-Based Costing (ABC)
- Cost allocation and drivers
- Equivalent units calculation

**Investment Evaluation:**
- Decision-making process
- Net Present Value (NPV)
- Financial and incremental logic
- IRR, Payback Period, Profitability Index
- Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) methods
- Sunk costs and avoidable costs

</details>
